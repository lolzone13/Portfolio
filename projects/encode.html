Bus Tracking Website

Developed in Encode Hackathon 2022

About the project

This project was developed in the Encode Hackathon 2022 conducted by ECell IIT Guwahati. The main idea behind the project was to enable people to track public busses and book seats in them. The idea was also pre notify people about the crowd in the in the bus so that they can take the decision of boarding it based on the covid situation at that time. The Poratl had 2 sides One for the cutomer and one for the driver. We also developed a app for the same

The frontend of the website was made with React.js and the backend of portal was made with Django Rest Framework. An OCR model for Driving license verification and an YOLO model for counting the number of people in the bus was developed


My Contribution to the project

  1. I participated in the developement of both the frontend and the backend of the website
  2. In the frontend I worked on the homescreen search screen and ticket booking screen
  3. I also worked on the bus search functionality and also on other components like the navbar etc
  4. In the backend I worked on the driver side of the website and developed the models and API's for that.
