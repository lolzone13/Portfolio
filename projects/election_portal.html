Election Portal

Developed under Student's Web Committee IIT Guwahati

About the project

Election Portal was a portal build on React for conducting transparent Gymkhana Elections. The aim of the project is to conduct fully transparent and secure elections in a college setup using Blockchain. Backed by Django, the portal used RESTful apis to authenticate users using Microsoft Oauth2.0. The ballots are securely transacted on a Ethereum based test network

Everything related to the elections was managed on this portal from registration of candidates to casting of votes and declaration of results. Candidates could create their profile page on the website on which they could post their manifesto and also share it among it among other students
