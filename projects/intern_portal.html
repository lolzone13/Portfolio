Intern FTE Portal

Developed under Coding Club IITG

About the project

This project was built to solve the problems faced by startups to hire from campus outside the official intern season. The start ups need to approach a club secretary who will then forward the message to the students. We decided to build a portal where companies could post opportunities and students can apply to the same.

The portal is built with React.js for the frontend and Node.js for the backend.


My Contribution to the project

  1. I worked on the designs and the frontend of the portal.
  2. Developed several pages like the public profile for companies and students and also the jost postinf forms and dashboards for both companies and students
  3. I also worked on the integration of the frontend with the backend
  4. My team also demonstrated the project in my college's Tech project showcase named Techivence 2022 to judges from several companies like Toshiba, Saptang Labs etc
